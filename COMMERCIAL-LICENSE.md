# Commercial licensing

Loadstone is source-available under the [Business Source License 1.1](LICENSE). In
plain terms:

**Free, forever, for almost everyone.** You can run Loadstone in production, at any
scale, for your own organization's workloads — commercial or not — at no cost. Each
released version automatically converts to the Apache 2.0 license four years after
release.

**Two uses need a commercial license:**

1. **Managed service** — offering Loadstone (or a service whose primary value is
   Loadstone) to third parties as a hosted product.
2. **OEM / embedding** — shipping Loadstone inside a commercial product or service you
   sell to third parties.

## Contact

For commercial licenses, support agreements, or anything unclear about whether your use
needs one: **open a GitHub issue with the `licensing` label** or email the maintainer
(see the repository profile). If you're unsure, ask — the answer for internal use is
almost always "you're fine, it's free."
